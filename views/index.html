Purpose of Presentation.

This presentation explores the question of "What will library technology collaboration in Colorado look like in five years?" by using the scenario forecasting model developed by futurist Jamais Cascio. In his model, we are asked to examine a single question in four scenarios.

For each scenario, we will ask what are our expectations in 2018 and what as leaders do we need to do now to avoid the worst outcomes while striving for the best outcomes.

  • The future is what I expect
  • The future is better than I expect.
  • The future is worse than I expect.
  • The future is weirder than I expect.
